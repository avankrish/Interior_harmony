Farmhouse Living Room Style

Farmstyle living rooms are trendy and known for their characteristic features like low-ceilings and exposed beams. The colours are usually neutral like beige, tan, dark brown, white etc. with a little colour from the accessories. You can choose to venture beyond the usual rustic theme and keep it stylish with a little help from the architect. The best part is that you can combine two different styles like transitional with traditional, industrial with contemporary etc. to create your own personal space.
