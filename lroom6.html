Contemporary Living Room Design

This living room design idea is filled with the right mix of colours and textures to bring out the best in the area. Adding different types of lights in the corners brighten the room and elevate its beauty at the same time. You can go for carpeted flooring in a colour that coordinates with the walls of the room.
